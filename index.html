Overview

This document provides verified evidence of HCL Technologies’ successful delivery of a critical project for TRW Automotive in Malaysia during 2011. The engagement highlights HCL’s proven ability to deliver mission-critical technology implementations within a constrained timeline and controlled budget.

Client Background: TRW Automotive

TRW Automotive, a global leader in automotive safety systems, steering, braking, and control technologies, partnered with HCL Technologies Malaysia to execute a critical enterprise systems implementation. The initiative demanded a partner with robust delivery governance, strong technical expertise, and the ability to ensure operational continuity in a production environment.
